The Dice

Different roll modes:

  1. Manual Stop - The roll button must be clicked once to start the roll and again to stop the roll
  2. Auto Roll - The dice stop rolling on their own after a slectable period of time, 1/10th of a second to 1 second

Dice location

  • The dice can be placed in two different places: against the roll button or on the top or left hand side depending on screen orientation.

Holding dice

  • After a roll is over, you tap on a die to hold it, once it fades, it is held, dice that are held do not change the next roll
  • When your turn is over, all the dice face indicating that you are to score that turn and the dice are final
