The Dice

Different roll modes:

  1. Manual Stop - The roll button must be clicked once to start the roll and again to stop the roll
  2. Auto Roll - The dice stop rolling on their own after a slectable period of time, 1/10th of a second to 1 second

Dice location

  • The dice can be placed in two different places: against the roll button or on the top or left hand side depending on screen orientation.

Holding dice

  • After a roll is over, you tap on a die to hold it, once it fades, it is held, dice that are held do not change the next roll
  • When your turn is over, all the dice face indicating that you are to score that turn and the dice are final

 

Scoring

About the score options

  • A score must be selected for every turn
  • BASIC SECTION
    • The Basic Section consists of 6 categories, each of these categories is scored by adding the total of dice that match the category.
    • Â A 35 point bonus is earned with a minimum of 63 in the Basic Section, a score of at least 71 but no more than 77 earns a bonus of 55 points; and 78 or more, a bonus of 75 points.
    • The overall Basic Section score is these two scores added together
  • LOWER SECTION
    • 2 Pair Same Color
      • Two pairs of numbers the same color, fifth die doesn't matter.
      • Score: sum of all dice
    • 3 of a Kind
      • Three or more dice having the same number.
      • Score: sum of all dice
    • Straight
      • 1-2-3-4-5 or 2-3-4-5-6
      • Score: 30
    • Flush
      • All dice showing the same color.
      • Score: 35
    • Full House
      • A Three of a Kind and a pair; color is not important.
      • Score: sum of all dice + 15
    • Full House Same Color
      • A Full House showing one color.
      • Score: sum of all dice + 20
    • 4 of a Kind
      • Four or more dice showing the same number.
      • Score: sum of all dice + 25
    • Yarborough
      • Any combination.
      • Score: sum of all dice
    • 5 of a Kind
      • All five dice showing the same number
      • Score: sum of all dice + 50
